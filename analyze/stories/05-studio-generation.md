# Story 5: Studio Generation & Quota Management

## Business Need
Trigger Studio features. Think of these as "App Store Cards"—highly visual and desirable.

## UI Elements & Interactions
- **Studio Cards:** Soft-edged cards for each feature (Audio, Flashcards). Each card has a specific SF Symbol (e.g., `waveform` for Audio).
- **The "Countdown" Overlay:** If a quota is hit, a soft blur covers the card with a "Ready in 04:59" timer.
- **Download Badge:** A tiny "Ready" badge with a download arrow appears on the card when finished.

## Designer Notes (Apple Minimalism)
- **Soft Shadows:** Use very light, diffused shadows for cards to give them depth.
- **Hover States:** Cards should lift slightly or glow when hovered.
- **Simple Toggles:** Use the standard iOS-style toggle switches.
